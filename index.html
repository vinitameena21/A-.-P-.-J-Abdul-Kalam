A . P . J Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Missile Man of India

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He came from a poor family and worked hard to study Aerospace Engineering.He became a top scientist at ISRO & DRDO, leading India's missile program. In 1998, he played a key role in India’s nuclear test.In 2002, he became India’s 11th President and was loved by all. He inspired students and worked for education and technology.On 27 July 2015, he passed away while giving a lecture. He won many awards, including Bharat Ratna.His famous quote: "Dream big and work hard to achieve it.

Biographies

  • Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He came from a poor family but worked hard to study Aerospace Engineering.
  • He became a top scientist at ISRO & DRDO, leading India's missile and nuclear programs. Because of his contributions, he was called the "Missile Man of India".
  • In 2002, he became India’s 11th President and was loved by all. He inspired students and worked for education and technology.
  • On 27 July 2015, he passed away while giving a lecture. He received many awards, including Bharat Ratna, India’s highest civilian honor.
